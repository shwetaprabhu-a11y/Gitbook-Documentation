__  01  PRODUCT SUMMARY__

Recent Activities provides a real\-time log of all significant account events since the member's last login — transfers initiated, payments made, login events, alert triggers, and form submissions\. It serves as an immediate session security audit, allowing members to quickly verify that no unexpected actions occurred since their previous session\.

My Insights delivers personalised spending analytics and financial intelligence based on the member's transaction history\. Transactions are automatically categorised by merchant type, enabling members to see spending breakdowns by category \(groceries, dining, utilities, entertainment\), income vs\. spending trends, and cash flow patterns\. The Insights tab provides category\-level drill\-down with individual transaction detail\.

Together, Recent Activities and My Insights give members both a security\-focused retrospective \(what happened\) and a financial intelligence view \(how money is being spent\) — two complementary lenses on their account activity\.
