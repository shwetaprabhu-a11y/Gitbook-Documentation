> **01 PRODUCT SUMMARY**

The External ACH Transfer feature allows members to send or pull funds between their credit union accounts and external bank accounts at other financial institutions via the ACH (Automated Clearing House) network. This is essential for members who maintain accounts at multiple banks, need to fund their CU account from an external source, or make payments to external obligations.

External transfers require identity verification for first-use or high-value transactions. A one-time passcode (OTP) is sent to the member's registered mobile number or email to authorise the transaction before it is submitted to the ACH network. External accounts must first be added via Recipient Management before they can be used as transfer destinations.

The ACH network processes transfers in 1–3 business days under standard ACH rules. Same-day ACH (SDACH) may be available for transfers submitted before the daily cut-off time, subject to CU policy and fee structure.
