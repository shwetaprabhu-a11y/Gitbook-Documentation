**Reorder Card**

Module: nFinia Digital Banking \> Cards \> Card Details \> Order a New Card

| 01   PRODUCT SUMMARY |
| :---- |

Reordering a card refers to requesting a new physical card for an account where a card already exists — typically because the card is worn, the magnetic stripe is damaged, or the chip is no longer working, but the card has not been lost or stolen. You can reorder a card using the Order a New Card feature in online banking.

When you reorder, the current card remains active until the new one arrives and is activated. A digital version of the new card is made available instantly, and the physical replacement is mailed to your address within 5 days. Set a PIN for the new card during the order process.

| 02   KEY USE CASES |
| :---- |

| Use Case | Who Uses It | What They Do | Business Value |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Replace a worn card** | Member whose card's magnetic stripe or chip no longer works reliably | Uses Order a New Card to reorder a replacement | New card issued with the same account; old card active until new one is activated |

| 03   STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE |
| :---- |

*Navigation: Log in to Summerville Credit Union online banking. Navigate to Cards, select your card, then click Order a New Card. Alternatively, use the Visa Debit / ATM Card Order Request form for non-OLB requests.*

**Step 1 — Arrive at the Dashboard**

After logging in, you land on the Dashboard showing your account balances, recent payments, and quick-action shortcuts. Navigate to Cards in the top navigation bar, then select the account for which you need a replacement card.

![Step 1: Dashboard — starting point for navigation][image1]

*Step 1: Dashboard — starting point for navigation*

**Step 2 — Complete the Card Order**

From Card Details, click Order a New Card. Select your account, card type, and design. Choose whether you want a physical card mailed to your address. Enter and confirm a new 5-digit PIN. Click to confirm your order. A digital card is available immediately and the physical card will arrive within 5 days.

![Step 2: Order a New Card form — complete details to reorder your card][image2]

*Step 2: Order a New Card form — complete details to reorder your card*

| Note: After your new card arrives by mail, activate it using the Activate Card feature in online banking before attempting to use it for transactions. |
