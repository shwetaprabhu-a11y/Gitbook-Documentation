**Order New Card**

Module: nFinia Digital Banking \> Cards \> Card Details \> Order a New Card

| 01   PRODUCT SUMMARY |
| :---- |

The Order New Card feature allows you to request an additional card for your membership account directly through online banking. You can choose the account, select a card type and design, opt for a physical card mailed to your address or a digital-only card, and set a PIN — all in a single workflow.

A digital version of the new card is made available instantly upon order completion. If you request a physical card, it will be mailed to your address on file within 5 days. This feature is useful when you want an additional card for a family member or a new card for a specific account.

**At a Glance**

| Attribute | Detail |
| :---- | :---- |
| Module | nFinia Digital Banking \> Cards \> Card Details \> Order a New Card |
| Card Design | Multiple design options available (carousel selection) |
| Delivery Options | Digital only (instant) or Physical card mailed within 5 days |
| PIN Required | A 5-digit PIN must be set during the order |
| Related Features | Activate Card, Set PIN, Card Controls |

| 02   KEY USE CASES |
| :---- |

| Use Case | Who Uses It | What They Do | Business Value |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Order a card for a new account** | Member who opened a new account | Selects the account, picks a design, sets delivery preference and PIN, submits | New card issued digitally with physical card to follow if requested |
| **Get an additional card** | Member wanting a second card for a family member | Completes the order form with the desired account and design | Additional card issued for the same account |
| **Digital-only card request** | Member who prefers not to receive a physical card | Selects No, just digital for delivery preference | Card is available digitally immediately with no physical card mailed |

| 03   STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE |
| :---- |

*Navigation: Log in to Summerville Credit Union online banking. From the Dashboard, click Cards, select your card, then click Order a New Card.*

**Step 1 — Arrive at the Dashboard**

After logging in, you land on the Dashboard. Click Cards in the top navigation, select your card from the list, and look for the Order a New Card option in the Card Details view.

![Step 1: Dashboard — click Cards to access your card list][image1]

*Step 1: Dashboard — click Cards to access your card list*

**Step 2 — Fill In the Order Form**

The Order a New Card form opens with four sections. (1) Select the account number the card should be linked to. (2) Select a card type from the dropdown and choose a visual card design from the carousel — multiple designs are available. (3) Choose whether you want a physical card mailed to your address or a digital-only card. (4) Enter and confirm a 5-digit PIN for the new card.

![Step 2: Complete all four sections of the Order a New Card form][image2]

*Step 2: Complete all four sections of the Order a New Card form*

**Step 3 — Review Options Selected**

Review your selections before submitting. Confirm the account, card type, design choice, and delivery preference are correct. If you need to make changes, update any field before proceeding. Once satisfied, review the summary and continue to the confirmation step.

![Step 3: Review your card order details before confirming][image3]

*Step 3: Review your card order details before confirming*

**Step 4 — Confirm Your Order**

A summary confirmation screen appears showing the delivery method — "A digital version of the new card will be made available to you instantly. Your new card will be mailed to your address within 5 days." Click Yes, Order a New Card to submit, or No, Cancel to return without ordering.

![Step 4: Confirm your order — digital card available instantly, physical within 5 days][image4]

*Step 4: Confirm your order — digital card available instantly, physical within 5 days*

**Step 5 — Order Complete**

An order completion screen confirms that your new card has been ordered. Your digital card is now available in your Cards list. If you requested a physical card, it will arrive at your mailing address within 5 days. You can return to Cards or the Dashboard using the buttons provided.

![Step 5: Order complete — your new card is ready to use digitally][image5]

*Step 5: Order complete — your new card is ready to use digitally*
