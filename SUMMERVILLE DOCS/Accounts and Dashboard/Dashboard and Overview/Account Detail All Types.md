__  01  PRODUCT SUMMARY__

Each account type in nFinia has a dedicated Account Detail page providing the full operational view of that account\. The Checking Account Detail is the most\-visited page for most members — it shows the real\-time balance, complete transaction history, and provides direct access to all account services\. Savings and dividends accounts add dividend rate information and earning summaries\.

Money Market accounts show tiered rate details alongside transaction history\. Certificate of Deposit accounts display maturity date, interest rate, term, and renewal options\. Loan accounts show the outstanding principal, next payment due, payment history, and provide direct access to the loan payment workflow\. Line of Credit accounts display the credit limit, drawn amount, available credit, and transaction history including draw and repayment records\.

All account detail pages follow a consistent layout: balance summary at top, transaction history in the main panel, and related actions/links in the side panel or at the bottom\. This consistency reduces the learning curve for members managing multiple account types\.

The member begins at the Dashboard after logging in\. The Dashboard displays all account balances, upcoming payments, quick\-action tiles, and the top navigation bar with links to Accounts, Move Money, and More\.
